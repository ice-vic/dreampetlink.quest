About the Game

Dream Pet Link is a captivating puzzle game that challenges players to connect matching pet icons with lines. The game features colorful graphics, cute animal characters, and progressively challenging levels that will keep you entertained for hours.

Whether you're looking for a quick casual game or a longer puzzle-solving session, Dream Pet Link offers gameplay that adapts to your style. The intuitive interface makes it easy for players of all ages to pick up and enjoy.

  • Hundreds of challenging levels
  • Cute and colorful pet characters
  • Smooth and responsive gameplay
  • Suitable for players of all ages

How to Play

Connect Matching Pets

Click on two identical pet icons to connect them with a line. The line can't have more than three turns.

Beat the Clock

Complete each level before the time runs out to advance to the next challenge.

Earn Stars

Complete levels with remaining time to earn up to three stars and unlock special content.

Gameplay Tips

  • Start from the edges of the board and work your way towards the center.
  • Look for long chains of matching pets to clear multiple pairs at once.
  • Use power-ups strategically to help you when you're stuck on a difficult level.
  • Take your time and plan your moves carefully to maximize your score.
